FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services.

How long does it take to complete a task?

Typically, it takes 2 to 5 business days to execute and complete a mission. However, the exact timeline depends entirely on the nature of the work and the specific location within India. We will give you a confirmed timeline before we begin.

How do I know you won’t take a bribe or commission from the supplier?

We are 100% independent. We do not sell products, manufacture goods, or accept side-deals from anyone in India. We are strictly on your payroll, and our only job is to protect your interests and bring you the unedited truth.

How do you share updates with me?

You get real-time updates via WhatsApp, Email, or Slack while we are on the ground—including live video calls on demand. At the end of every task, we deliver a comprehensive final report packed with raw photos, videos, hard facts, and our professional observations.

Can I hire you for just a single small task?

Yes, absolutely. We have no long-term contracts or minimum commitments. You can hire us for a single-day personal errand, a quick package pickup, or a multi-day commercial factory audit. You only pay for the specific mission you need.
